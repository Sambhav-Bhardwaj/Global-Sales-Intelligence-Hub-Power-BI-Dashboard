Global Sales Intelligence Hub (Power BI)

An end-to-end Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI, designed to deliver executive-level insights into sales performance, product trends, and customer & regional behavior.

This project demonstrates strong skills in data modeling, DAX, interactive visualizations, and business insight generation.

Project Overview

The dashboard is structured into three interactive tabs, each focused on a critical business perspective:

Tab 1: Executive Sales Overview

Provides a high-level snapshot of overall business performance.

Key Highlights:

Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Order Value, Total Quantity Sold

Monthly Revenue Trend analysis

Product-wise sales comparison

Customer contribution analysis

Country-wise sales distribution (Map)

Tab 2: Product Performance Analysis

Focused on evaluating product-level profitability and demand.

Key Highlights:

Total Units Sold & Total Revenue

Average Product Price

Best Performing Product (Top Product Code)

Sales & Quantity by Product Line

MSRP vs Sales relationship (Bubble Chart)

Detailed product-level performance table

Tab 3: Customer & Region Insights

Analyzes customer behavior and regional sales patterns.

Key Highlights:

Total Customers & Average Sales per Customer

Top Customers by Revenue

Sales by Territory & Country

City-wise sales comparison

Customer detail table with drill-down capability

Key KPIs & DAX Measures Used

Total Sales

Total Orders

Total Quantity Sold

Average Order Value (AOV)

Average Price

Top Product by Sales

Average Sales per Customer

All KPIs are built using custom DAX measures to ensure accurate aggregations and dynamic filtering.
